# Contributing to Megathread

Thank you for your interest in contributing. Megathread is intended to help and educate people; it is not intended to promote, facilitate, or enable piracy or other illegal activity. By contributing you agree that your contributions comply with these guidelines.

## Our purpose
We aim to provide helpful information and tools for people who lack resources, focusing on legal, ethical, and educational uses. We do not condone or support piracy or copyright infringement.

## What we accept
- Bug reports, feature requests, and improvements that do not promote or enable illegal distribution of copyrighted material.
- Documentation improvements, tutorials, and resources that focus on lawful access, open-source alternatives, and legal remedies.
- Code contributions that respect third-party licenses and do not include proprietary or infringing content.

## What we do NOT accept
- Any contribution (code, documentation, links, or media) that is intended to enable, promote, or facilitate piracy or unlawful distribution of copyrighted works.
- Downloads, links, or instructions that bypass licensing, DRM, or other access controls to obtain copyrighted material illegally.

## How to contribute
1. Fork the repository and create a feature branch from main (or the repository's default branch).
2. Make your changes with clear, focused commits.
3. Open a pull request describing the change, why it's needed, and any relevant references.

If your contribution could be interpreted as potentially facilitating access to paid or copyrighted material, please provide context and a clear explanation of lawful, ethical use-cases.

## Licensing & attribution
By submitting a pull request you agree to license your contribution under this repository's license. Do not include third-party files or code unless you own the rights or clearly state the license and you are permitted to relicense under the repository license.

## Reporting problematic content
If you see content in this repository or in a contribution that appears to promote piracy or otherwise illegal activity, please open an issue or contact the maintainers immediately. We will review and remove content that violates these guidelines.

## Code of Conduct
Be respectful. Harassment, hate speech, or abusive behaviour will not be tolerated. Treat contributors with courtesy and patience.

## Questions
If you're unsure whether something is appropriate to contribute, open an issue describing what you want to add and we'll discuss it with you.
